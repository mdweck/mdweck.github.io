The company that I will be choosing is high end swiss watch company. The name of the company is Audemars Piguet. The company was founded in 1875 by two watchmakers. The two men that created the company were Jules-Louis Audemars, and Edward-Auguste Piguet; Hence the name Audemars Piguet. The Swiss company designs and manufactures highly complex mechanical,and automatic watches. The brand is present in 88 countries.Audemars Piguet has been a manufacturer since 1875, making it one of the world's oldest watch manufacturers. The company is still owned by its founding families. The company is widely known throughout the world. The Audemars Piguet watch group is composed of 1,200 employees, 14 distribution subsidiaries, and 16 boutiques worldwide. It comprises three production sites which are Le Brassus, Le Locle and Meyrin in Switzerland. The company introduced many trends that are still followed today,which started with one of their most iconic models which are still popular today.


The Royal Oak was characterized by a revolutionary design in the form of a rounded octagon. At the time, luxury watches on the market were all round, slim and in gold. The Royal Oak was the first high-end sports watch made of steel, recognizable by its octagon bezel with eight hexagon white gold screws. On 6 September 1651 Oliver Cromwell's troops pursued King Charles II, the future king of England, Scotland and Ireland. To escape them, he took refuge in the branches of an oak tree. The tree became the Royal Oak and a symbol of power and protection.


Many famous celebrities and athletes have been seen publically wearing the watch including Arnold Schwarzenegger and Muhammad Ali.
